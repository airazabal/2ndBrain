Architecture Highlights
🔄
P2P
Device Sync
Google Nearby Connections syncs screen time & sessions between phone and tablet — no Wi-Fi or internet required. Background sync every 15 min via WorkManager.
🗄️
100%
Local Storage
All data lives in an on-device Room database. 5 entities, 17 schema versions, strict migrations — your data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync.
⚡
3
Background Workers
UsageSyncWorker (4 h), P2pSyncWorker (15 min), ReflectionWorker (6 h) — scheduled via WorkManager, running silently in the background 24/7.
🔒

Private by Design

2ndBrain stores everything locally in an encrypted Room database on your device. API keys are secured with Android EncryptedSharedPreferences. P2P sync uses direct Bluetooth/Wi-Fi Direct — your personal data never passes through a cloud server.
